USD to MXN Transfer Guide

US to Mexico is the largest migration corridor in the world, with billions of pesos sent home every month. Maximizing the amount of pesos that arrive in Mexico requires careful consideration of provider markups and pickup conveniences.

1. Bank Deposit vs. Retail Cash Pickup

For cash pickup in Mexico, providers like Ria, Western Union, and MoneyGram partner with major Mexican chains like OXXO, Elektra, Coppel, and BanCoppel. These offer great convenience but often have slightly lower exchange rates or higher fees than bank-to-bank electronic transfers.
