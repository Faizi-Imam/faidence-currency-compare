Frequently Asked Questions

How do we calculate the exchange rates?

We fetch the interbank mid-market exchange rate in real-time. For each provider, we calculate their customized rate based on their historical rate markups (the difference between mid-market and what they sell to customers) and add their flat or percentage fees. This gives you the most accurate real-cost comparison available.

Which provider is usually the cheapest?

For bank-to-bank transfers, Wise typically offers the lowest fees and closest exchange rates to the mid-market rate. For cash pickups, Ria or MoneyGram are usually cheaper than PayPal, which carries heavy markups. Always compare real-time rates for your specific amount and corridor before sending.

What is a hidden exchange rate markup?

Many providers claim to offer "Zero Fees" or "No Commission" transfers, but they hide their actual charges by offering an exchange rate that is significantly worse than the real mid-market rate. This markup is a hidden fee. Our table computes this markup dynamically, showing you the real, honest cost of your transfer.
